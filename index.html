Featured Products

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans. It has a rich, intense flavor and is the base for many other coffee drinks like lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos. A good espresso has a layer of crema on top, which is a frothy, golden-brown foam that adds to its unique taste and texture

Latte

A latte, or caffè latte, is made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. It is creamy and smooth, often enjoyed with various flavorings such as vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut. Lattes can also be made with alternative milks like almond, soy, or oat milk for different flavor profiles and dietary preferences.

$21.99

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a popular Italian coffee drink that consists of equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It has a bold coffee flavor balanced by the sweetness and creaminess of the milk. Cappuccinos are often garnished with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon, adding an extra layer of flavor.

$23.99

Cold Brew

Cold brew coffee is made by steeping coarsely ground coffee beans in cold water for an extended period, usually 12-24 hours. The result is a smooth, less acidic coffee with a naturally sweet flavor. Cold brew is often served over ice and can be mixed with milk or sweeteners. It's a refreshing option for hot days and can be enjoyed black or with various flavorings.
